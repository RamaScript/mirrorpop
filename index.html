What is this

A pocket mirror that lives where you already look.

MirrorPop turns your phone's front camera into a floating mirror app for Android, docked on your lock screen and unfolding from the notch in a clean rounded panel. There’s no app to open and no photo ever taken — just a fast, private glance whenever you need one.

Instant overlay

The mirror appears over the lock screen in under 100ms — before you've finished the press.

Notch-anchored mirror

A clean, rounded front-camera preview that unfolds from the notch. No filters, no controls, nothing to get in the way.

Fully offline

No cloud, no backend, no login. MirrorPop never needs an internet connection to work.

Nothing saved

The camera stream is never recorded, written to disk, or shared. It exists for a moment, then it's gone.

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 6, 2026 · Applies to the MirrorPop Android app

The short version: MirrorPop doesn't collect, store, or transmit anything. There is no server to send data to, because MirrorPop doesn't have one.

What MirrorPop does

MirrorPop is a lock-screen utility that shows a live front-camera preview inside a rounded overlay anchored to the notch when you press and hold a floating bubble. The preview exists only while you're holding the bubble and disappears the moment you release it.

What we collect

Nothing. MirrorPop has no account system, no backend server, no database, and no analytics software of any kind built in. The app was built to work entirely on your device, with or without an internet connection.

  • The camera stream shown in the mirror is never recorded, saved, or written to storage.
  • MirrorPop makes no network requests — there is nowhere for your data to go, even if we wanted it to.
  • We don't know who you are, how often you use the app, or what you look like. We built it that way on purpose.

Permissions we ask for, and why

Android requires MirrorPop to request a few permissions to work the way it's designed to. Here's exactly what each one is used for:

Permission What it's for
Camera Shows the live front-camera preview inside the mirror panel. The feed is never recorded or saved.
Draw over other apps Lets the floating bubble and mirror overlay appear on top of your lock screen.
Accessibility Service Used only to detect the lock-screen state so the bubble can appear reliably. It does not read screen content or other apps' data.
Foreground Service Keeps the lock-screen bubble available and responsive, and lets Android show you that MirrorPop is active.

You can review or revoke any of these permissions at any time from your device's Settings app. Doing so may limit or disable the bubble, but won't affect anything else on your phone.

Third parties

MirrorPop doesn't integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics tools. No data is shared with anyone, because none is collected in the first place.

Children's privacy

MirrorPop does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children, since it collects no information at all. The app is a general-purpose utility not directed specifically at children.

Data retention

There is nothing to retain. Camera frames are processed only in memory to render the live preview and are discarded immediately after each frame is drawn.

Changes to this policy

If MirrorPop's approach to privacy ever changes, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change. We'll always aim to keep the app as data-light as it is today.
